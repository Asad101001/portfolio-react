Urdu Legal Assistant

LegalEaseAI

Understanding legal documents shouldn't require a law degree. Built in 72 hours for the HEC Generative AI Training Cohort 2, LegalEaseAI processes dense, technical English contracts and provides instant, plain-Urdu explanations, risk ratings, and a precise RAG Q&A chatbot.

The Problem

Pakistan has over 220 million people, and millions sign agreements daily written in English legalese they cannot understand. Lawyers charge PKR 5,000–50,000 per consultation—a week's wage for most. This gap leads to exploited workers and trapped tenants.

The Solution

Users upload any legal document (PDF, DOCX, TXT) to instantly get plain-Urdu explanations of every clause, color-coded risk ratings (High/Medium/Safe), and a highly-optimized conversational chatbot grounded strictly in the provided document using Vector DBs.

System Architecture

[Client Uploads Document]
       │
       ▼
[Extraction] -> pdfplumber / python-docx
       │
       ▼
[Chunking] -> LangChain RecursiveTextSplitter (Optimized for Legal Clauses)
       │
       ▼
[Vectorization] -> Cohere/OpenAI Embeddings -> FAISS (In-Memory Vector Search)
       │
       ▼
[Analysis] -> Keyword/Regex Risk Classifier + Gemini Prompt Engineering
       │
       ▼
[Inference] -> Groq LPUs (Llama 3) for zero-latency RAG response generation

Core Technologies

Python 3.11+ FastAPI LangChain FAISS Memory Groq LPUs
