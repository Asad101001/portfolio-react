How to get the images:
  1. Open LinkedIn → Licenses & Certifications
  2. Click "Show credential" on each cert
  3. Screenshot the certificate page
  4. Save with the exact filename above

To add more certs:
  1. Drop the image here (e.g. new-cert.png)
  2. Open index.html, find "ADD MORE CERTS HERE"
  3. Copy a cert-card block and update src, title, issuer, date, ID
